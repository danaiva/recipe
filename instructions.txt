Favourite Recipe: Roasted Air Fryer Chickpeas

Ingredients:
> 1 can of chickpeas
> 1/2 tablespoon of oil
> 1/4 teaspoon pepper
> 1 teaspoon paprika
> 1 teaspoon garlic powder
> 1 teaspoon onion powder 

Steps
1. Drain chickpeas, rinse in cold water and pat dry
2. In a large bowl, combine dried chickpeas, oil, pepper, paprica,
   garlic powder and onlion powder and stir until combined
3. transfer mixture to air fryer and roast for 12 minutes at 390F. 
4. serve with spicy mayonnaise.
